Report Description: On 4/16/23 at about 1830 (DOB Arkansas
DCFS) reportedly called for help after slipping and falling as she got into the shower. She
stated that she felt like her ankle twisted and it was in pain. The medical provider was
contacted, and an x-ray was ordered of the right ankle. The x-ray came back negative for
fracture or soft tissue swelling.
Interim Action Narrative: Medical provider was contacted and an x-ray was ordered.
Maltreatment Narrative:
Licensing Narrative: Licensing Specialist reviewed provider reported incident. Licensing
Specialist will follow-up with facility on prevention. 5/30/2023, Licensing Specialist
inquired about safety measures to prevent future slips in the shower. 6/1/2023, Licensing
Specialist contacted the facility for an update on the safety measures. Licensing Specialist
informed that residents are provided shower shoes to wear if needed. Staff are to check the
bathroom in between residents to make sure there is not excess water or anything on the
floor to slip on.
