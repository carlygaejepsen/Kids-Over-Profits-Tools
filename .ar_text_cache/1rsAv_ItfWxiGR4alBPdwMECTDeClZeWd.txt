Licensing Specialist completed buildings and grounds on the following houses: Chestnut, Sturgis, Rose, and

where residents keep their personal items that they check out when needed. Both houses were clean and were
decorated for the holiday. Residents had their bedroom door decorated for the holiday also.

Licensing Specialist observed a welcome wreath on the door of the houses. Licensing Specialist observed the
following documentation on the walls: residents’ rights, food menu, washing hands instructions, emergency
escape plan, chore list, activity calendar, shower times and which shower to use.

Licensing Specialist will follow-up with facility regarding the light cover plates.
