staff being aware of the number of clients they are supervising. Sweatpants with string was immediately

Dean Hill reported that the units occasionally have 9 residents assigned on them in which the nurse would
work in ratio with the direct care staff assigned to the unit that night. The Coordinator discussed that if the
nurse is to be counted in ratio then they will be needed to work in ratio on the unit with visual of the residents.
The Coordinator discussed how watching video at the nurses station of the units or not visualizing the
residents does not count as working in ratio for the supervision of the residents.
