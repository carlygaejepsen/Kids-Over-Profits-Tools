Licensing Specialist completed buildings and grounds on the following the areas: gym, units (East, West,
North, and South), gym, computer lab, classrooms, cafeteria, dining room area, game room, garden area, and
courtyards.

Grounds were clean and free of debris. Licensing Specialist observed the main courtyard and the west
courtyard. Licensing observed a staff member and residents walking through the courtyard, 1:3. Licensing
Specialist observed the playground equipment, two areas where residents can play basketball, and the ga ga
pit (outdoor game). The west unit courtyard was once the garden area.

Licensing Specialist observed residents transitioning to their classrooms, they were coming from lunch.
Licensing Specialist observed classrooms (1, 2, 3, 4, and 5). Some classrooms were empty due to some
residents being in the gym or the game room. Each classroom, game room, and gym were in ratio. The facility
has two (2) game rooms, the music room is now both for music and games.

Licensing Specialist observed the residents’ rights, program rules, activity calendar (April), and evacuation
plans on the walls of the classrooms and units. All units (East, West, North, and South) were clean with the
aroma of cleaning products. Bedrooms were organized and the bathrooms had running water.

Licensing Specialist was informed by Mr. Justin Hoover (CEO) the residents must display safety to participate
in gardening. The garden is behind a locked fence near the main courtyard. Mr. Hoover informed Licensing
Specialist that as of 3/1/2023, the facility has been seclusion free. The seclusion rooms are being used as
storage closets. North Unit had residents on the unit ratio 1:3.
