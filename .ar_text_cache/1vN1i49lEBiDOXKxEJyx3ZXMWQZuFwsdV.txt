Licensing Specialist completed a buildings and grounds walkthrough of Mabee, Rose, Chestnut, and Sturgis
houses. Licensing Specialist was escorted by Mr. Jon Pulliam, Lead Unit Manager. Licensing Specialist

Specialist walkthrough.

walkthrough. Bathrooms were clean and free of debris. All bedrooms were organized, beds were either made

residents to have drinking water. Licensing Specialist observed laundry in the milieu that was being folded.

Mabee houses.
