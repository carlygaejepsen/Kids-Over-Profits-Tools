Report Description: On September 5, 2023 an incident occurred at EMCC located at 6601
West 12th Street in Little Rock AR. Client, was in
a verbal argument with other peers while at recess. Staff made the decision to move all
clients back to the classroom. Client, walked to the playground and flipped
over a picnic table then walked around the playground. Staff, began to walk to
the playground to get client , and client went under the
slide, staff then attempted catch client when he began
running from under the slide. Staff chased client to the
fence where it appeared that client was pinned against the fence by staff
. Staff then grabbed client and drug him
approximately 20-30 feet across the ground by his left arm. When client
and staff got back to the top of the stairs near the building, client
began running towards a inside corner of the building. Staff began
chasing client and went out of camera view as soon as they reached the
inside corner of the building. Client, then hit the building with his left arm
and appeared to be holding the arm when he came back into camera view. Staff
then took client inside to the nursing station. Client,
was accessed by nursing staff where it was determined that client
would be taken to Ortho Arkansas to be treated. Later client, was taken to
Arkansas Childrens Hospital. After an x-ray it was determined that client '
left wrist was fractured. After treatment client was released back to The
Centers.
