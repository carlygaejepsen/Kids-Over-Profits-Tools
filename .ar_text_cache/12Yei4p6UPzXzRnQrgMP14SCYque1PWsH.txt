Primary Narrative By Corlan Taylor, 08/07/25 17:27
Runaway Attempt
On August 7th,​ at approximately 1727 hours,​ Deputy Parker and I were dispatched to the youth home located at ​20400
COLONEL GLENN ROAD in reference to two juvenile females ​running away from the facility.
​
Upon arrival,​ Deputy Parker and I made contact with Joseph Davis (caller). Davis escorted us to the two juveniles
identified as Haylee ​Pischke ​and Audrey Grantham. Pischke ​and Grantham informed Deputy Parker and I that they are
in a relationship and that the reason for them running off was because the staff was wanting to separate them from
each other. The two juveniles were yelling and shouting,​ and not wanting to listen to what Deputy Parker,​ myself and
the staff were trying to say. The staff made a final decision to separate the two juveniles for safety precaution and due
to ​the relationship that they were vocalizing. Grantham was escorted to the Crestview house. Deputy Parker and I
helped the staff escort Pischke ​to her living area after she refused numerous times and stated ​that she wasn't going to
the area she was being assigned to. The juveniles only got a short distance ​up the street from the facility in their
attempt at running away before returning with Davis. Neither of the juveniles were harmed or required medical
attention. Both of them eventually cooperated and went to their assigned living quarters. ​
​
I gave this report number to a staff member and advised if they had any issues,​ questions or concerns to contact the
Sheriff's Office. Nothing further to report. ​
