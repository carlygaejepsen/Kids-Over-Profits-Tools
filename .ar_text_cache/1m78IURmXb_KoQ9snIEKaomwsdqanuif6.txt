Facility visited on 3/12/25 in response to complaint 028130 that staff was physically aggressive
towards a client during an incident that occurred 3/8/25 at Rock Hill Cottage. Nursing note for dates
3/5/2025 through 3/9/2025 and witness statements from the resident, a peer, and two staff were
provided by the facility to licensing. A third-party staff witness statement describes that staff alleged
to have been physically aggressive “pushed him back mildly”, him referring to resident. Staff was
placed on administrative leave pending the completion of the investigation.
The staff named in this incident completed TCI training on 10/28/24. All background checks for this
staff were completed and approved. As a precautionary measure, the named staff will complete
retraining on TCI.
Licensing reviewed video of incident that took place on 3/8/2025. The staff/client ratio was reported
to be 2:10 at the time of the incident by Chris Butler as not all residents could be seen on the video
footage by the licensing specialist. Staff named in the complaint can be seen escorting the resident
to his room by grabbing one of resident’s arms, under the arm and gripping on the forearm. The
staff then sits in a chair outside of the resident’s room in a doorway that is visible on video. Behind
the doorway is the resident’s bedroom which is not visible in video. While the staff is sitting outside
the bedroom, the resident can be seen getting close to staff in an aggressive manner, putting his
face close to hers. The named staff is seen pushing in the resident’s direction as if to create space
between herself and resident. For a brief period, the staff is completely out of view in video, having
walked in the direction of resident’s room. The named staff then returns to her chair outside the
bedroom.
