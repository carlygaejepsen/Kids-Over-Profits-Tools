Program Coordinator met with Paul Hofstad and Barbara McCrory to discuss accommodation for the residents
at EMAC while flooring is being replaced. The facility does not have an exact timeframe of how long it will

The facility presented two (2) options:
· For a two (2) day operation, the flooring company would strip the floors on day one (1). The residents’
