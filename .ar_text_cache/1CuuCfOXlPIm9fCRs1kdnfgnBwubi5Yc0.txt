injured his arm while playing basketball and was transported to Drew
Memorial Emergency Medical Center. was then transferred to Children's Hospital, and
it was determined that had a break on the thumb side of the hand, lying laterally in the
lower arm. A cast and sling were secured. Facility visited 8/17/23 and video reviewed of
incident. Client is seen playing basketball, inside, in the cafeteria, The cafeteria tables
are folded up against the wall. Staff/client ratio: 2:7. The area where these tables are folded
up against the wall inspected during today's visit. The tables fold up and can be secured
inside a box which is fastened to the cafeteria walls with hex bolts connected to a concrete
wall. The box was securely fastened to the wall today. Continuing in the video, pulls on
a table which is secured in a box against the wall. The box and the table fall on client .
The box was not securely fastened to the wall. Facility cited 911.6: All buildings and
furnishings shall be clean, safe and in good repair. There would be no indication that these
boxes are not securely fastened to the wall unless pulled. Additional hex bolts will be added
to the fold-away table boxes as soon as possible. Maintenance has been notified of this.
