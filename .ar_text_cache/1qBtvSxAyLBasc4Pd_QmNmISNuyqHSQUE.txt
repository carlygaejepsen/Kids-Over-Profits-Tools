Program Coordinator Slade was escorted throughout the facility by Mr. Morris and Mr. Williams.

The following areas were observed: grounds, administration, gym, cafeteria, residents’ hall including the

residents were present on the grounds. The Maintenance Supervisor was observed sitting outside on the
courtyard while the contractors were maintaining the grounds inside the fence.

Mr. Williams informed Program Coordinator that a roofing company was replacing the roof. Per Mr.
Williams, the roofing company are about 95% complete. Program Coordinator observed roofing materials on
the opposite side of the fence out of reach from the residents.

Entering the gym, Program Coordinator observed residents playing basketball, sitting on the benches, walking
around the gym, and a resident was observed laying on a bench. The ratio for the gym was 3:15.

Program Coordinator could smell the aroma of cleaning products throughout the residents’ halls. On the 400
Hall the ration was 1:6. The residents were participating in downtime. Residents were observed in their rooms
resting (laying on their beds awake), reading a book, or talking to staff/peers.

Walking to the cafeteria, Program Coordinator could hear music. The residents were eating desserts provided
by the facility. Ratio 4:18, staff were observed throughout the cafeteria. Residents were observed sitting down
at the table eating or communicating with peers. Other residents were observed dancing and singing songs
with staff.

was provided.
