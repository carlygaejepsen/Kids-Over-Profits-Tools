Report Description: Willful failure of a mandated reporter to notify child abuse authorities of
known child maltreatment contrary to Arkansas state statute 27-18-201
Interim Action Narrative:
Maltreatment Narrative:
Licensing Narrative: 10/6/2023- Program Coordinator spoke to the reporter and gather
more information about the complaint. The reporter stated that the resident returned home
from a weekend pass with her non-custodial parent and was reportedly acting "strange".
RN "Donald" reportedly drug tested the resident and she came back positive for marijuana.
The reporter suspected that the non-custodial parent provided the resident with the
marijuana as he has a known history of substance abuse. The reporter states that the facility
failed to call the child abuse hotline and instead stated that they will be doing their own
investigation internally. The family has filed a police report with the Pulaski Co. Sheriff's
office for the facility staff's failure to notify the child abuse hotline in a timely manner. The
incident occurred on 10/1/2023 and it is reported that no call to the child abuse hotline has
been made by the facility as of 10/6/2023. 10/9/2023, Licensing Specialist requested
nursing notes for 10/1/2023. Licensing Specialist inquired of lab results had been received.
