to occur. Documentation will be required as a part of the new CAA).

Unit on 6/7/2022 regarding the use of showers at the facility until all repairs are made
to the bathrooms to ensure the safety of all residents. All staff should be trained on the
new Emergency Safety Procedure immediately and a list of all staff trained shall be
provided to the Licensing Unit by 6/24/2022. (Completed)

harm by residents to the Licensing Unit by the next business day for the duration of this
Corrective Action Agreement. (In compliance)

the Minimum Licensing Standards. All facility staff shall be required to attend and a sign
in sheet will be provided to the Licensing Unit. (Completed. In addition, the Licensing
Unit also provided Intent Training on sections 107, 108, 109, 110, and a second
training on the 900 Section of the Minimum Licensing standards on 12/20/22 and
12/21/22).
