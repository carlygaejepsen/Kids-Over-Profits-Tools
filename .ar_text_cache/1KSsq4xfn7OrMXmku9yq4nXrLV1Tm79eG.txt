wanted to make a report about an incident.
When I arrived I made contact with a female later identified as Mia Velasquez (H/F DOB: .)
I asked Mia to tell me what happened. Mia stated that she was watching over the patient and one of them got
a towel. The patient then started wrapping it around her neck in an attempt to harm herself. Mia said that

the patient punched and kicked her in the face. Mia was able to get the towel away from patient and
restrained her. While the patient was restrained, she kicked Mia again causing her to stumble backwards and
heard a pop in her upper neck.

decided to go to the hospital. Another officer followed Mia to the hospital while I remained on scene to get the
suspects information.
