Finding Description: A physical restraint ordered for one resident on 12/15/2024 at 1635 was signed by the
physician three business days later on 12/18/2024. The current Corrective Action Agreement requires that the
facility ensure all physician orders are signed within two business days following the order being given.

Action Due Date:

Action Due Description: The facility will continue to monitor all physician orders to ensure they receive the
required signature by the ordering physician within two business days following the order to maintain compliance
with the current Corrective Action Agreement.
