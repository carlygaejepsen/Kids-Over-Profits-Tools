Licensing Specialist Norton and Program Coordinator Rice completed buildings and grounds for Elizabeth
Mitchell Centers: EMCC. Mr. Paul Hofstad, Director of Risk Management; Mr. Cole Mitchell, Chief Strategy
Officer; and Mr. Mason, Program Manager; escorted licensing throughout the buildings and grounds.

The following areas were observed: therapy hallway, laundry room, dining room, New Dorm, East Dorm,
South Dorm, bathrooms, “cozy corner”, game room, and playground.

The grounds of EMCC were well maintained, clean, and free of safety hazards. Program Coordinator and
Licensing Specialist observed employees walking to and from buildings.

No residents were observed in the dorms of EMCC. North Dorm was not observed due to a therapy session
being conducted with two residents. (Ratio 1:2) All bedrooms in New Dorm, East Dorm, and South Dorm
were clean and organized. All dorms smelled of cleaning products and were free of safety hazards. The
bathroom in East Dorm was clean and sanitary, with an adequate supply of hot and cold water.

The laundry areas, dining areas, and therapy hallways were clean and free of safety hazards. The playground
area was mostly clean, but a few pieces of trash were observed in the grass. Maintenance was contacted
on-site to remove the trash and arrived before licensing left the facility.
