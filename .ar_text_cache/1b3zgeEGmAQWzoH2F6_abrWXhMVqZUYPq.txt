Regulations Needing Technical Assistance:
Regulation Number: 1. 102. 3 .h
Regulation Description: The following policies of the agency shall be current and available to all employees of
the agency and the Licensing Specialist:
Findings Description: It was determined that the agency's current policy on mandated reporting requires staff to
"notify their supervisor immediately to determine need to report, reporting method and whom the report is to be
submitted, what immediate work needs to be done with the child, and what work needs to be done with the
perpetrator." This is not in compliance with current Arkansas state laws on reporting which state "An employer or
supervisor of an employee identified as a mandated reporter shall not require an employee or a volunteer to obtain
permission or notify any person, including an employee or a supervisor, before reporting child maltreatment to the
Child Abuse Hotline".
Action Due Date: 4/7/2023

Action Due Description: Please ensure that your agency reviews your current mandated reporter policy and
update it to ensure that it complies with Arkansas state law on mandated reporting. Please ensure a copy of the
new policy is provided to the licensing unit by 4/7/2023 and all staff are retrained on the new policy by 4/21/2023.
