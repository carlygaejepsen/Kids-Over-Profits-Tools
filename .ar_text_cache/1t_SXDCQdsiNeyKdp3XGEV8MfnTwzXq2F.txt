Date of Visit: 9/26/2023

Visit Type(s): Consultation Visit

Visit Comment :

Licensing Program Administrator JJ and Program Coordinator J met with facility
staff to discuss concerns regarding recent compliance with the Minimum Licensing Standards.

The facility reported that they have developed several strategies to improve their compliance and services.
The facility discussed how they have hired two staff to now work directly with reporting and tracking
compliance with different regulatory agencies, a new staff training position was created and filled to work
directly with staff across all shifts, they have changed who is responsible for interviewing potential candidates
for their direct care staff and updated the preferred requirements for their direct care staff to include a
bachelor's degree. The facility reports that they are aware of deficits in their program and actively working to
correct them but implementing the above.

Licensing will continue to monitor the progress of the facility and its compliance with the Minimum
Licensing Standards. Manager explained that an update will be given to the CWARB on October 25th
at 1:30PM regarding the recent compliance concerns and encouraged staff to attend so they can discuss their
plans on how to regain compliance.
