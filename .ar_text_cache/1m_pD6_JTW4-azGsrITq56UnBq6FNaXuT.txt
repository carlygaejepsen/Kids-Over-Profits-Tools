Narrative:

On 01-16-23 Resident ma used a wooden piece from broken sink counter in Room 201 to self-harm and
create superficial wound to arm. Facility will be cited for standard 911.6--all buildings and furnishings shall
be safe, clean, and in good repair. Technical assistance was provided during licensing walkthrough on
01-09-23 to ensure this area was repaired with a note made that repairs were pending. This incident occurred
one week later on 01-16-2023. Please ensure that all repairs are completed timely, especially when a broken
furnishing could potentially be used as a self-harm implement. If there is a delay in getting replacement
materials, please ensure the safety of the area while repairs are pending--e.g. increased supervision, limited
access, etc.

Facility will also be cited for standard 907.2--childcare staff shall be responsible for providing the level of
supervision, care, and treatment necessary to ensure the safety and well-being of each child at the facility,
taking into account the child's age, individual differences and abilities, surrounding circumstances, hazards,
and risks. Please ensure there is increased supervision in any area with identified safety hazards and please
ensure that children who are on constant LOS/self-harm precautions are supervised heavily in areas with
identified safety hazards.

Licensing viewed room 201 during unannounced buildings and grounds visit on 01-19-2023 and found that
the sink area in Room 201 has been repaired and the lining has been secured.
