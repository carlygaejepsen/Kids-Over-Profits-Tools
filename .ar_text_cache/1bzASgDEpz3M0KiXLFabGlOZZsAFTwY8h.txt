Program Manager and Program Coordinator met with the facility staff to include Rachel Howard, Paul
Hofstad, and Barbra McCrory. During the meeting, licensing discussed recent complaints and citations
regarding staff/child ratio.

The facility staff recognized that they were struggling to maintain staff and experiencing a high level of call
in's particularly on the 3pm-11pm shift. In response, the facility has already implemented a 60-day plan that is
set to end on 6/2/2024. The internal plan for the agency was to implement a bonus/overtime plan for staff,
implementing a new hire bonus, Rachel Howard is also now assisting with some of the hiring duties, two
recreational staff now work over to assist with the 3pm-11pm staff to provide activities for the children, The
facility anticipates being fully staffed by the end of 5/2024. The facility reports they have had no issues with
staff/child ratio on the units since mid April.

Licensing explained that we will continue to work with the agency and monitor their progress with hiring and
maintaining staff to ensure staff/child ratio is kept on all shifts.
