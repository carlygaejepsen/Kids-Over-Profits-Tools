The licensing specialist and program coordinator visited the facility to discuss the initiation of a formal
Corrective Action Agreement between the facility and DHS. The Program Coordinator presented the
Agreement to Dean Hill, Lachandra Williams, Becky Brooks, Lashundra Kesee, Dede Smith and Emily
Greene.
The facility reports that they have recently installed new sensors on the end of each of their resident units. The
staff have a wand that they must carry to the end of each hall and wave in front of the sensor so it can log that
staff were present on the unit. The information is then saved on the device and can be downloaded by staff the
following morning to ensure staff are completing their required visual checks of the residents during sleeping
hours. The facility has installed motion sensors on the end of the halls to alert staff when a resident has come
off a unit. The facility has also already completed two in service trainings for staff on the importance of
documentation and supervision of the residents on 2/16/23 and 3/1/23. Sign in sheets were provided to the
specialist.
