Licensing Specialist completed a buildings and grounds walkthrough of EMAC at Elizabeth Mitchell Centers.
Licensing Specialist observed the following areas: treatment hall, dayrooms, comfort room, rage room,
hygiene closets, library, bedrooms, bathrooms, cafeteria, sand tray room, art therapy room, nurse station, and
grounds.

All four (4) dorms were cleaned. Each bedroom and bathroom were organized and free of debris. Licensing
Specialist observed plants on the window seals in some of the bedrooms. Licensing Specialist was informed
that the plants were part of therapy (seed digging therapy technique). Clients’ rights and emergency escape
plans were observed on the doors or walls of each dorm. Licensing Specialist observed the garden by the cozy
corner.

In two of dayrooms, Licensing Specialist observed where residents are working on puzzles. The hygiene
closets were organized and only staff are allowed in the closets. The grounds were clean with no debris.
Licensing Specialist observed recreational activities for the residents to utilize.

Licensing Specialist observed three (3) classrooms. The bathrooms near the classrooms were clean and free of
debris. Classroom | ratio 3:7, Classroom 2 ratio 3:8, and Classroom 3 ratio 3:7.

Licensing Specialist observed the nursing station. The nurse showed Licensing Specialist where medication is
kept. All medications are kept in a locked closet inside of a metal med cart. The nurse also showed Licensing
Specialist where controlled substances are kept. There are no residents on any controlled substances.

All rooms and areas observed by Licensing Specialist were organized, clean, and free of debris.
