Narrative -Johnson, James Ryan -9/3/2023 6:01:39PM(Initial)
Behavioral Unit for three runaway male juveniles. Upon my arrival, employees of
Millcreek were directing me down the road to where other employees had one
juvenile on the ground. Once I got to those employees, they pointed to other
employees who had the second juvenile caught. After making sure they had the
second juvenile, it was told to us that the third male juvenile ran into the woods
across the street from Industrial Drive. Officer Murry and I made contact with the
juvenile about 50 yards from the roadway and secured him in handcuffs. We
escorted him to my patrol unit, where he was placed in the backseat and taken
to his dorm. After dropping him off, I went back and took the first juvenile that
was caught back to the same dorm. All three juveniles were dropped off at the
dorm, and no further incidents happened.
