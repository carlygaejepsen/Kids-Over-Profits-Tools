Licensing received a complaint on 1/29/2025 for ELS Case #027163.
Licensing Specialist Horton met with Rachel Howard and Barbara McCory at the facility to review camera
footage for this complaint. Rachel reported to Licensing that the resident told her that a direct care staff
member got on to her and had her stand in the corner in the classroom. Rachel reported she asked the resident
if the direct care staff member had hurt her or her peers. Resident reported yes and that the direct care staff
member has hurt peers. Rachel asked resident what that meant and resident reported that the direct care staff
member bent residents’ fingers and wrist back. Rachel reported that she asked the resident if the direct care
staff member had done this to her and resident reported yes while she was standing in the corner when she got
in trouble.
Licensing observed the resident sitting at her desk while the direct care staff member was teaching. It
appeared to Licensing that the resident was talking with peers in the classroom while the direct care staff
member was teaching. There was no sound on this video footage. Licensing observed the staff member walk
towards the resident and then escorted her to the front of the classroom to an area out of camera view. The
ratio was 5 to 14.
The facility reported that the staff member has been suspended pending further investigation.
Licensing is not prepared to leave a finding at this time.
