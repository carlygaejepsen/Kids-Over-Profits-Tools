Provider Comments: While we acknowledge that there was an unfortunate use of crude language there are two issues we need to point out.
First there are extenuating circumstances that we feel should be considered. The youth in this situation was actively
attempting to inflict injury on the staff by grabbing the staff members already injured wrist. The staff member was trying
to escape the grip and was in obvious discomfort if not significant pain. A small slip in terms of language in such a
circumstance while not acceptable, would certainly seem to be mitigated by the severity of the assualt and pain inflicted.

from pain and possibly frustration at being assualted. The staff member clearly instructed the client to let go, then asked
her to please let go before pulling away and making the comment. The comment in itself was in no way disciplianry and
did not involve any potential consequence regarding the child's behavior. We would therefore respectfully request that
