Narrative:
Licensing Specialist Michele Sutton and Program Coordinator Chelsea Vardell reviewed personnel records for
11 staff to include
The facility will be cited for the following issues:
105.3- All pre-service documentation was not documented in all 11 personnel records.
105.13- Training documentation shall include the date, hours, topic, and source. Training records for all 11
personnel only contained the date and topic of the training. Please ensure that the source and hours for each
training are clearly documented in the files.
105.10- Each personnel shall have 10 hours of psychiatric specific training before being counted in the
staff/child ratio. All 11 personnel records viewed today did not have 10 hours of psychiatric training
documented.
107.4- Personnel records should include documentation that they have been made aware of the need to protect
the confidentiality of children in the use of social media. The documentation was only found in 1 of the 11
personnel files viewed today. Please ensure that documentation of the personnel's education of the social
media policy is in the personnel files.
103.2- Personnel AR Child Maltreatment regulatory check was not initiated within ten days of hire.
(CEO Cassie Sowder reports that this was likely due to the termination of the facility's HR Director 1/9/23).
105.15.h- 10 of the 11 personnel records viewed today did not have three references from non-relatives in the
personnel file.
The facility received Technical Assistance for the following issues:
105.15.c- T/A- Please ensure that all personnel have some form of verification of their qualifications. For
example, high school diploma, GED, or college diploma.
