DPSQA - Complaint
Report Description: An anonymous report was received regarding the ratio, supervision, and
quality of care of resident at the PRTF program. The complainant reported that nursing
staff are frequently being pulled away from their nursing duties to be used as ratio staff as
there are often not enough direct care staff (BHAs) to maintain ratio. The complainant
states that nursing staff are unable to complete their normal job duties and maintain the
required documentation for nursing duties. The complainant reported that the agency is
cutting BHA staff and relying on nurses to be counted in ratio instead of maintaining or
hiring more BHA staff to provide direct care for the residents. Additionally, when nursing
needs assistance from BHA staff to help escort or draw blood from a resident, no one is
available because of the lack of staff. The complainant is concerned that with the agency
relying more on nurses to count in ratio, the nurses will be unable to provide the medical
care necessary to the residents.
