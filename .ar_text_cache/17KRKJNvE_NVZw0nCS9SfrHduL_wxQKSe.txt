Licensing received a provider reported incident on 11/23/2024 for ELS Case #025730.
Licensing Specialist reviewed camera footage for this provider reported incident with Ms. K. Barrett. The
incident took place in the boy’s foyer unit with a ratio of 2:1.
It was reported on 11/23/2024 at around 4:53 p.m. the resident was observed laying and crawling on the floor
of the boy’s foyer. Ms. Barrett reported to Licensing that the resident was in the boy’s foyer area for being
disruptive. The resident could be heard on the video making loud annoying noises as he moved around in the
boy’s foyer.
Licensing observed the resident moving close to the staff member’s personal space. The staff member and the
resident were viewed having a physical altercation.
Another staff member did come in the foyer when the resident began yelling and screaming but failed to seek
additional assistance during the incident.
The facility was issued two citations. 109.1.g a male staff member was observed being inappropriate and
907.2 a female staff member did not provide adequate supervision to ensure safety of the resident.
