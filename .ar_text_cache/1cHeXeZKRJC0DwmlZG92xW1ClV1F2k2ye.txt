Licensing Specialist completed buildings and grounds on the PRTF’s.
Walking the grounds, Licensing Specialist heard and observed residents playing basketball. The grounds were
wet due to the rain and well-kept with no debris.
Licensing Specialist was escorted by Jon Pulliam, PRTF Lead Unit Manager. Licensing Specialist observed
the following houses: Sturgis, Chestnut, Rose, and Mabee. Licensing Specialist observed the following areas
of the houses: bedrooms, bathrooms, day areas, hallway, laundry, game rooms, milieu, and dining area.
Sturgis House: Ratio 1:1, other residents were out playing basketball and sitting under the pavilion. Licensing
Specialist could smell the aroma of the house being recently cleaned. Chestnut House: Residents were out
playing basketball and sitting under the pavilion. Housekeeping was cleaning the house during the
walkthrough. Licensing Specialist observed the Summer Daily Schedule posted on the wall.
Rose House: Ratio 3:12. Residents were present during the walkthrough. Some residents were in the game
room and day area watching television. Mabee House: Ratio 3:12. Residents were present during the
walkthrough. Some residents were watching television in the day area and talking with staff. Licensing
Specialist observed one resident in her bedroom laying down.
Licensing Specialist observed the emergency escape plans and children’s rights posted on the walls in
different locations of the houses. Fire extinguishers were observed on the walls behind a case. All bedrooms
were clean and organized. The bathrooms were clean with running water.
Mr. Pulliam informed Licensing Specialist that Chestnut House will be moving to Hickory House on July 5th,
2023, while the house (Chestnut) is being repaired. Residents at Hickory House have moved to Cartwright
House.
