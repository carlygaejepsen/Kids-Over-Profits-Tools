Program Coordinator reviewed camera footage with Ms. Barbara McCrory. This incident happened at EMAC
in a classroom.
Program Coordinator observed residents sitting at desks and standing up communicating with one another.
The resident named in this provider reported incident was standing up communicating with a peer. The peer
that attacked the resident was observed standing in front of a desk talking with a peer. The ratio was 2:8, one
staff member was sitting at a desk in front of the classroom and the other staff member was out of view of the
camera toward the back of the classroom.
The peer was observed walking up to the resident and started punching her with closed fists in the resident’s
shoulder up area. Program Coordinator observed peer punching the resident eleven (11) times before staff was
observed intervening between the two residents. From the angle of the camera, it appeared that both staff
members were not able to separate the residents due to being blocked by residents and chairs being near the
area where they were sitting.
Once the staff member from the back of the classroom separated the two residents the staff member in the
front of the classroom was observed on a walkie talkie while helping to assist. The resident was walked to the
classroom door by the staff members with the peer following close behind the staff members. The peer was
observed punching in the direction of the staff members and the resident.
Other staff members were observed at the classroom door. Staff were able to get the resident out of the
classroom. The other residents that were in the classroom were escorted out and the peer remained in the
classroom, ratio 5:1. Program Coordinator observed staff communicating with the peer.
Before this incident, peer was on assault and elopement precautions. After this incident, peer was also placed
on 10-foot restriction. Ms. McCrory informed Program Coordinator that the classroom would be rearranged to
assure that staff members are able to respond to situations accordingly.
