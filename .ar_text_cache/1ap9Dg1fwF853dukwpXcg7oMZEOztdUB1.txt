Primary Narrative By James Tallent, 06/21/25 13:47
Runaway
On 6-21-25 at approximately 1347 hours Deputies were dispatched to 20400 Colonel Glenn Road to investigate the
report of four juveniles running away from the Colonel Glenn ​Youth ​Home.
​
As the call was being dispatched I was in front of the address and did not see any juvenile running. Contact was then
made with staff and I was advised that there had been at least a five minute time lapse from the juveniles running and
the phone call to 911 being made.
​
I then proceeded ​east from that location to the Area of Talley Ho Trail and ​Angela Drive where an unsecured trailer
was located. The residence was cleared with negative result in making contact with the juveniles.
​
After canvassing the area deputies returned to the Youth Home Where contact was made with Brittney Hubbard and
she stated that she was the subject that had called 911 to report the juveniles running away. stated
that she was on the basketball court with when a
basketball ​was thrown over the fence of the basketball court. stated that when the four juveniles went to
get the basketball all four of them ran east into the wooded area and disappeared.
​
While collecting information for the report staff advised that someone had seen the four juveniles walking on
Burlingame Road north of Stewart Road. Both myself and Deputy Washing went to the area and all four juveniles were
located just south of Top Hill Road. The juveniles were advised to stop walking in which they complied.
​
Deputy Washington transported two juveniles as I transported the other two back to the Colonel Glenn Youth Home.
The juveniles were turned over to Ms. Hubbard
