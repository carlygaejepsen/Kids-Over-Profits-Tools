Report Description: Please see the attached report concerning Little Creek Behavioral
Health (LCBH), the psychiatric residential treatment facility (PRTF) located in Conway,
Arkansas. The oversight gap created by allowing this exclusively out-of-state resident
facility to be licensed in Arkansas has led to significant compliance issues that affect the
safety and well-being of the children residing there. Prior to considering any forthcoming
requests by LCBH to expand their capacity we hope that you will carefully review the
attached report. Key findings from the attached review include: ? Widespread deficiencies in
the use and documentation of physical and chemical restraints were found, with numerous
instances of noncompliance with federal regulations. ? Inadequate treatment planning,
including delays in creating individualized treatment plans, lack of sufficient or appropriate
treatment goals, and failure to timely or meaningfully review plans and patient care. ?
Concerns related to medication management ? Overuse of punitive measures such as unit
restrictions that are being applied frequently, inconsistently, and without clear
documentation. The attached document provides a detailed analysis, including evidence
from facility records, interviews with staff, and a review of resident charts. It also addresses
the implications of these deficiencies for the planned expansion of LCBH?s operations.
