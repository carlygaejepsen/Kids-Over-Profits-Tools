Licensing Specialist completed a walkthrough of Centers/Destiny House with Eric Knowles, Compliance
Officer. Licensing Specials observed the following areas: grounds, dining room, kitchen, bedrooms hallway,
administration hall, and bathrooms.
The grounds were clean, and no debris observed. Licensing Specialist observed a picnic area for the residents
to sit outside when the weather permits. The flooring on the administration hall was being replaced. Licensing
Specialist informed that residents are not allowed on the hall while the flooring is being replaced. The dining
area was clean, and the smell of cleaning products was detected.
No residents were in the bedrooms or in the area. The residents were in class during the walkthrough.
Bedrooms and bathrooms were clean and organized. The bathrooms had hot and cold running water.
Licensing Specialist observed the client rights and emergency escape plan on the walls or by the doors.
Licensing Specialist did not observe any licensing concerns during the walkthrough of the Destiny House.

Licensing Specialist completed a walkthrough of EMCC.
EMCC incurred a leak on 12/24/2022 causing water damage to parts of the building. The day area and North
Dorm sustained major damage. Licensing Specialist observed the following areas, day area, nurse’s station,
North, South, and East Dorms. Flooring and based boards were replaced in all dorms, nurse’s station, and the
day area including the hall.
North, South, and East Dorms, the bedrooms and bathrooms were clean. No debris observed from the
reconstruction. The bathrooms had running hot and cold water. On the North Dorm, the sign on the girls’
bathroom door was slightly lifted. Maintenance corrected on site. The ceiling has been replaced in the day
area and the North Dorm. No wires were observed hanging from the ceiling.
The reconstruction of EMCC meets the minimum licensing standards. The Little Rock Fire Department
completed the fire inspection at EMCC on 3/9/2023. Licensing Specialist received a copy of the fire
inspection.
Licensing Specialist and Mr. Knowles, Compliance Officer, discussed the process of the alternative
compliance being removed. Licensing Specialist will notify Licensing Managers of the reconstruction
completion of EMCC.
