Narrative - Mccracken, Joel - 2/14/2025 8:34:02 PM (Initial)
On 2/14/25 at 1411 hours Detective Morgan and I were dispatched to 211 N Church in reference to a
resident that was out of control and threatening staff. When we arrived on scene we made contact with staff who
stated that juvenile 1 was at the end of the hall around the corner. They stated he had ripped the thermostat off the
wall and had the screws in his knuckles using them as a weapon. I and officer Morgan went down the hall to speak
with juvenile. He was at the end of the hall very upset sitting on the floor. He was complying with our requests. He
showed me his hands and stated he did not have the screws anymore, that staff got them all from him. Juvenile 1
stated that he was upset because the other kids were bullying him and calling him fat. He stated that the staff doesn't
do anything to make them stop. This is what juvenile 1 said was the start of the incident.
From Incident reports received from staff by Detective Morgan, Juvenile 1 became upset in the day room and
broke a dvd player and projector. Upon being taken back to his room Juvenile 1 pulled the thermostat off the wall
using the screws that secured it in his fists with the sharp points out. Then Amanda Goodwin was then threatened
directly. Juvenile 1 with screws in his fist, sharp points out, threatened to hit Amanda. He then charged at her. In
Trevin Johnson statement he stated he heard juvenile 1 say "Leave me alone" and "he would fucking kill everyone".
Juvenile office was called. I spoke with Amy over the incident. The staff member in charge at the facility
stated that they wanted him to go to jail. After approval from Amy, juvenile 1 was transported to Craighead county
detention center juvenile side.
